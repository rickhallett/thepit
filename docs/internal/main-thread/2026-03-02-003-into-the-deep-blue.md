# Into the Deep Blue

**Date:** 2 March 2026
**Register:** Wardroom
**Participants:** Captain, Weaver

---

## Context

Stage Magnum executive order (SD-278) has been committed to chain. The pilot study is officially over. All strategy documents are on file. The gate has been honourably retired (SD-285). The Captain printed the five strategy planks and presented briefly to his parents. Standing outside, birdsong, holding the papers — the first true stop since project inception.

---

## Weaver's Note

The branch that was master — that carried 420+ PRs, 847 commits, 277 session decisions, 1,289 tests, 16 fight card entries, 13 agents, and one human through 384 hours of the most determined ascent — is renamed `wake`.

The wake is the trail a ship leaves in the water. You can see where she has been. You cannot follow her there. It fades. The sea closes over it. But the ship that made it is still sailing.

`paragate` is the working branch. The voyage continues.
