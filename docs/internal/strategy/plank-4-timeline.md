# Timeline — Strategy and Day-by-Day Playbook

## Strategy Overview

### Why this sequence

The job market for AI safety/evaluation roles operates on two timescales simultaneously. The first is immediate: specific roles with open headcount and, in one case, a hard deadline (DeepMind FTC, 10 March). The second is medium-term: community presence that generates inbound interest over weeks.

The strategy runs both tracks in parallel. Track 1 is direct outreach to open roles — applications, direct messages, the one-page story. Track 2 is community presence — publish the build-reflect piece, engage on Alignment Forum and LessWrong, follow and reply to researchers on X/Twitter.

Track 1 is high-stakes, low-volume. Track 2 is lower-stakes, compound interest.

The Captain's time is the bottleneck. Every action below is designed to take less than 2 hours of human time. Agent time handles research, drafting, and formatting. Human time goes to editing, personalising outreach, and making judgment calls about tone and fit.

### Why Anthropic first

Not because it's the dream — because the exchange is most mutual. You stress-tested their model. Your data is material to their safety mission. The fight card is an artifact they cannot generate internally. Leading with Anthropic signals ambition without arrogance because you're offering something, not just asking.

### Why the build-reflect piece matters

It converts a private project into a public credential. The one-page story is for hiring managers. The build-reflect piece is for the community. The community surfaces your name to the hiring managers you can't reach directly. This is not content marketing — it is showing the work.

## Parallel Track: Exploration Continues

The Captain's nature is to explore. This is not a distraction from the job search — it feeds it. Each new finding (correlation analysis, narrative-layer pipeline, slopodar entries) becomes content that reinforces the public profile. The constraint is time allocation:

- **80% of available time → Track 1 (applications + outreach)**
- **20% → Track 2 (exploration + content)**

This ratio inverts once the pipeline has 3+ active conversations. At that point, the exploration becomes interview preparation and the content becomes demonstrated ongoing capability.
