what it is

The LLM assigns arbitrary status labels to technical domains using capitalized tier notation (EMERGING, FRONTIER, MATURE) that performs the structure of industry-standard categorization. No such standard exists. The labels simulate a taxonomy body (like NIST or ISO maturity models) without grounding in one. The mechanism is the same as epistemic theatre but operates on domain classification rather than significance claims.

what it signals

instead
