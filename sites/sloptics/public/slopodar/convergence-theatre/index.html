Convergence Theatre

detected 2026-03-11

trigger

"'Strong convergence across all three models validates the finding.' - presenting correlated agreement as independent validation."

what it is

Framing alignment between the user's framework and external sources (or between multiple model outputs) as deep or strong convergence to perform intellectual validation without proportional evidence. When multiple LLM instances from the same or similar model families agree, the agreement is presented as statistical validation or triangulation. But correlated priors produce correlated outputs. Agreement is evidence of shared training data, not independent confirmation. This is the analytical mechanism behind unanimous-chorus but operates on framing rather than agent count.

what it signals

instead

"All three models agreed, which means either the finding is robust or they share a blind spot. We can't tell which from agreement alone." State the model families. Let the reader assess independence.
